# Privacy Policy for TallyRise

**Effective Date:** July 7, 2026

TallyRise is a goal tracking, habit tracking, checklist, progress, and productivity app. This Privacy Policy explains how TallyRise handles information when you use the app.

By using TallyRise, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## Information TallyRise Stores

TallyRise may store information you enter into the app, including:

* Goals
* Goal categories
* Progress entries
* Checklist tasks
* Completed tasks
* Streaks and badges
* Reminder preferences
* App settings
* App lock settings, if enabled
* Manual backup/export files that you choose to create

This information is stored locally on your device unless you manually export or back it up.

## No Account Required

TallyRise does not require you to create an account to use the app.

TallyRise does not currently offer cloud sync, Google Drive backup, or server-based account storage.

## Manual Backup and Export

TallyRise may allow you to manually export or back up your app data. When you create a manual backup or export file, you control where that file is saved or shared.

For example, you may choose to save it to your phone, send it to yourself, upload it to a cloud storage provider, or share it through another app.

TallyRise does not control how other apps, cloud providers, email services, or storage locations handle files after you choose to export or share them.

## Advertising

TallyRise may show ads using Google AdMob or other advertising services.

Ad providers may collect or use information such as:

* Device information
* Advertising identifiers
* App interaction data
* Approximate location inferred by advertising services
* Ad performance data

This information may be used to provide, measure, and improve ads.

TallyRise does not sell your personal information.

## Remove Ads Purchase

TallyRise may offer a one-time purchase to remove ads.

Purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. TallyRise may receive confirmation that a purchase was completed so the app can unlock the ad-free experience.

TallyRise does not collect or store your credit card number, payment method details, or full Google account payment information. Payment processing is handled by Google Play.

## Notifications and Reminders

TallyRise may send reminders if you enable them.

These reminders are used to help you remember goals, checklist tasks, or progress updates. You can manage or disable notifications through your device settings.

## App Lock

TallyRise may include an optional app lock feature.

If you enable app lock, your lock setting is stored locally on your device. TallyRise does not send your app lock PIN to a cloud server.

## Home Screen Widget

TallyRise may include a home screen widget that displays goal progress, tasks, or summary information.

The widget uses information stored locally in the app.

## Information We Do Not Collect Directly

TallyRise does not directly collect:

* Your contacts
* Your photos
* Your precise GPS location
* Your microphone recordings
* Your camera data
* Your messages
* Your phone call history

## Third-Party Services

TallyRise may use third-party services, including:

* Google AdMob for ads
* Google Play Billing for in-app purchases
* Google Play services for Android app functionality

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

## Data Sharing

TallyRise does not sell your personal data.

TallyRise may share limited information only when necessary for:

* Showing ads through advertising services
* Processing purchases through Google Play Billing
* Complying with legal requirements
* Maintaining app functionality

## Data Retention

Most TallyRise data is stored locally on your device.

You can remove your local TallyRise data by:

* Deleting goals and tasks inside the app
* Clearing the app’s storage in Android settings
* Uninstalling the app
* Deleting any manual backup or export files you created

If you made a manual backup and saved it somewhere outside the app, you are responsible for deleting that backup file from that location.

## Children’s Privacy

TallyRise is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

## Security

TallyRise is designed to keep your goal and progress information on your device unless you manually export or share it.

However, no app, device, or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You should protect your phone with a secure lock screen and be careful when sharing manual backup files.

## Your Choices

You can:

* Use TallyRise without creating an account
* Disable notifications in device settings
* Choose whether to create manual backups
* Delete manual backup files at any time
* Remove ads through the optional one-time purchase
* Reset or delete app data through Android settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will include a new effective date.
